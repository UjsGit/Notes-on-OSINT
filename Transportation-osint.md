In **India**: 

You can find details like owner name, RC status, insurance, challan and registration by their number plate (which is unique for every vehicle). 
  
   - **[Parivahan](https://parivahan.gov.in/)** is India's official centralized online portal for all vehicle-related services (Vahan) and driving license management (Sarathi), enabling citizens to handle RTO tasks digitally from home.    

   - **[Policy Bazar RTO](https://www.policybazaar.com/rto/)** find RTO details by registration number.
    
   - **[Car Info](https://www.carinfo.app/)** has several features but the key feature is you can extract info about vehicle via its Registration number which is in front and back of the vehicle.
