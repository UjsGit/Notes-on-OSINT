### â–¶ Profile Image Analysis:

Profile pictures are often reused across platforms by the same person.

Useful checks:
- Reverse image search (Google, Yandex, Bing, Shutterstock)
- Facial similarity search tools like [PimEyes](https://pimeyes.com/en) , [TinEye](https://tineye.com/)
- AI-assisted image similarity tools

With the rise of generative AI images nowadays, checking for:
- Unrealistic backgrounds
- Asymmetry artifacts
- Inconsistent lighting
- Absurd hand and legs
is increasingly important.

Fake or honeytrap accounts often use:

- Images taken from Pinterest or stock image sites
- Stolen influencer photos
- Overly polished AI-generated portraits
- Images taken from Reddit or Telegram Profiles

Reverse searching these images may reveal the original source.
